## **Q1**

### **Passage**

```
Heures d'ouverture du service consulaire :
Le service consulaire est ouvert au public les
lundis, mercredis et jeudis de 8 h à 14 h et les
mardis et vendredis de 14 h 30 à 18 h.

(Consular service opening hours:
The consular service is open to the public on
Mondays, Wednesdays and Thursdays from 8 AM to
2 PM and on Tuesdays and Fridays from 2:30 PM
to 6 PM.)
```

### **Prompt & Answers**

|  | French | English |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Prompt** | Quand le service est-il fermé au public ? | When is the service closed to the public? |
| A | Le lundi matin | Monday morning |
| **B** | **Le mardi matin** | **Tuesday morning** |
| C | Le mercredi matin | Wednesday morning |
| D | Le jeudi matin | Thursday morning |

### **Correct Answer: B -- Le mardi matin**

Tuesdays are only open from 14 h 30 à 18 h (afternoons). Tuesday MORNING is not listed -- the service is CLOSED then. Mondays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays all have morning hours (8 h à 14 h).

### **Why the others are wrong**

**A -- Le lundi matin.** Mondays are open 8 h à 14 h, which covers the entire morning.

**C -- Le mercredi matin.** Wednesdays are open 8 h à 14 h -- open all morning.

**D -- Le jeudi matin.** Thursdays are open 8 h à 14 h -- open all morning.
